Purpose:

The Blanding Utah Chamber of Commerce, located in Blanding, UT, is an organization of local businesses that promotes economic development and business activity in Blanding. The Chamber of Commerce encourages a positive business climate by working with investors, attracting visitors, and creating a strong community. As an important representative for the Blanding business and employer community, the Chamber of Commerce can also influence San Juan County public policy.
